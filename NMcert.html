NMIX 4220: Digital Brown Bag

In Digital Brown Bag, we had weekly seminars with new media industry professionals as guest speakers or a presentation on vital life skills that are typically not taught as college courses. The seminars helped me understand what factors make us stand out among other candidates, what kinds of questions to ask in an interview, and how to naturally develop career networks. From the presentations, I learned about creating business cards, searching and preparing for a specific job opening, managing credit cards, and understanding the nuances of health insurance. Although this course did not have projects, I want to share a couple of our interesting assignments.
