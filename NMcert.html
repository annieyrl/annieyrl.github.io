New Media Certificate

To be frank, the main reason why I started pursuing this certificate was to take the New Media Capstone class. Currently, the Computer Science department at the University of Georgia does not offer any capstone courses, which I believe would be a vital portion of my education and preparation for a job. Not only are capstones designed to showcase a personalized solution to a realistic problem, the result also reflects on the quality of each member's teamwork and leadership skills and their work ethic.

During my certificate journey, I learned how to develop iOS applications through the Rich Media Productions class, which complements my knowledge and experience in Android app development. I also had invaluable networking opportunities with industry professionals; took a peek at various corporate structures, values, and job positions; and gained fundamental financial literacy through the Digital Brown Bag class.
